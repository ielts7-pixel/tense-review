📚 國中英文時態複習

Concept Questions & Time Lines

考試倒數 3 天 - 掌握時態本質

ℹ️ 本講義採用Workman《Concept Questions & Time Lines》的教學方法，強調理解時態的本質含義而非只是記憶規則。

Present Simple

現在簡單式 (i) - 習慣與常規

I get up at 6:00 every morning.
我每天早上6點起床。
時態概念 The Present Simple is used to talk about routines or habits.
(現在簡單式用來表達習慣性或經常重複的動作)

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Is this something I do every day? （我每天都做這件事嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Is it a routine / habit? （這是一個習慣嗎？）
(Yes)
重點記憶 ✓ 時間詞：every day, always, usually, often, sometimes, never
✓ 第三人稱單數動詞加 -s / -es
✓ 表示客觀事實或永恆真理

Present Simple (ii) - 時間表未來

The train leaves at 6:00 on Monday.
火車週一早上6點出發。(時刻表上)
時態概念 The Present Simple is used to talk about future events on a timetable or programme of events.
(用來談論時間表或安排的未來事件)

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Is it on a timetable? （這在時間表上嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Does this happen at the same time every day? （這每天在同樣的時間發生嗎？）
(Yes)

Present Continuous

現在進行式 (i) - 現在正在發生

"What are you doing?" "I'm listening to some music."
"你在做什麼?" "我正在聽音樂。"
時態概念 The Present Continuous is used to talk about something that is in progress at the present moment.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Is this happening now? （這現在正在發生嗎？）
(Yes)
重點記憶 ✓ 結構：am/is/are + V-ing
✓ 時間詞：now, right now, at the moment
✓ 強調動作正在進行中

Present Continuous (ii) - 日記未來

I'm meeting Jane at 3:30 on Friday.
我週五下午3:30要見Jane。(已經確定安排)
時態概念 The Present Continuous is used to talk about definite future arrangements.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Are we talking about the present or the future? （我們談論現在或未來？）
(Future)
☐
2. Is it a definite arrangement? （這是確定的安排嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
3. So is it in my diary? （所以它在我的日記裡嗎？）
(Yes)

Past Simple

過去簡單式 - 過去完成的動作

I saw a good film last night.
我昨晚看了一部很好的電影。
時態概念 The Past Simple is used to talk about things that happened in the past, are finished and have no connection with the present.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Is the action in the past? （這個動作在過去嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Is the action finished? （動作完成了嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
3. Is there any connection with the present? （和現在有任何聯繫嗎？）
(No)
☐
4. Do we know when it happened? （我們知道它何時發生嗎？）
(Yes, usually)
重點記憶 ✓ 規則動詞：+ -ed
✓ 不規則動詞：go-went, see-saw, do-did, eat-ate
✓ 時間詞：yesterday, last week, ago, in 2020

Past Continuous

過去進行式 - 過去某時刻進行中

I was watching TV at 8 o'clock.
我在8點鐘時正在看電視。
時態概念 The Past Continuous is used to talk about an action that was in progress at a past point in time.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Did I start watching TV before 8 o'clock? （我在8點之前開始看電視嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Was I still watching TV after 8 o'clock? （我在8點之後還在看電視嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
3. So was this action in progress at 8 o'clock? （所以這個動作在8點時進行中嗎？）
(Yes)

Present Perfect Simple

(i) 過去對現在有影響 - 未完成的時間段

Have you seen Joe this morning?
你今天早上見過Joe嗎？
時態概念 The Present Perfect connects the past to the present. It shows that something happened in an unfinished period that still affects the present moment. It's not about whether the action is "finished" - it's about whether the time period is still relevant NOW.

核心：過去 → 現在的連結，不是「完成」

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Is this time period still continuing RIGHT NOW? （這個時間段現在還在進行嗎？）
(Yes - it's still morning now)
☐
2. Does it connect to the present moment? （它與現在時刻相連嗎？）
(Yes - that's the key!)
☐
3. What happens when we say it in the afternoon? （在下午說呢？）
(We say "Did you see Joe this morning?" - Past Simple - because morning is no longer relevant to NOW)

(ii) 未完成的過去 - 持續使用

She has worked here for 3 years.
她在這裡工作3年了。(還在工作)
時態概念 The Present Perfect Simple is used to talk about the duration of an action that started in the past and continues up to and includes the present.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Do we know when the action started? （我們知道動作何時開始嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Does the action continue up to and include the present? （動作一直進行到現在嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
3. Does she still work there? （她現在還在那裡工作嗎？）
(Yes)

Past Perfect Simple

過去完成式 - 兩個過去動作的先後順序

🔑 重點：過去完成式一定要有「兩個過去動作」
用來表示在過去的某個時間點之前已經完成的動作
When I arrived at the station, the train had already left.
當我到達車站時，火車已經離開了。
兩個動作：
① 火車離開 (had left) - 第一個發生 ← 過去完成式
② 我到達 (arrived) - 第二個發生 ← 過去簡單式
時態概念 The Past Perfect is used to talk about one action in the past that happened before another action in the past. It shows which action happened first.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Are both actions in the past? （兩個動作都在過去嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Did one action finish before the other action started? （一個動作在另一個開始之前完成了嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
3. So is the Past Perfect action the earlier one? （所以過去完成式的動作是較早發生的嗎？）
(Yes)
重點記憶 ✓ 結構：had + 過去分詞
✓ 用於表達過去時間中的先後順序
✓ 常與 Past Simple 搭配使用
✓ 時間詞：by the time, when, after, before

Future Simple

(i) 即時決定 - 臨時決定

"There are no buses today." "OK, I'll take a taxi."
"今天沒有公車。" "好吧,我搭計程車。"
時態概念 The Future Simple is used to express a decision made at the moment of speaking (spontaneous decision).

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Did I decide now or in the past? （我現在決定還是過去決定？）
(Now)
☐
2. Is it a spontaneous decision? （這是臨時決定嗎？）
(Yes)

(ii) 不可避免的事實

I'll be thirty in July.
我將在七月滿三十歲。(無法改變)
時態概念 The Future Simple is used to talk about inevitable future events - things in the future that cannot be changed.

✓ 檢查理解

☐
1. Are we talking about the future? （我們談論未來嗎？）
(Yes)
☐
2. Can I change this future fact? （我能改變這個未來的事實嗎？）
(No)
☐
3. Is it inevitable? （這是不可避免的嗎？）
(Yes)

關鍵時態對比

1️⃣ Present Simple vs Present Continuous

時態 例句 含義
Simple I eat dinner every day. 習慣性
Continuous I am eating dinner now. 現在進行

2️⃣ Past Simple vs Present Perfect

時態 例句 含義
Past Simple I ate lunch at 12 o'clock. 完全過去的事實
Present Perfect I have already eaten, so I'm full. 對現在有影響

3️⃣ be going to vs will

形式 例句 用途
be going to Look! It's going to rain. 已計畫或明顯將發生的事
will There are no buses. I'll take a taxi. 臨時決定

4️⃣ 完成式對比

時態 例句 核心含義
Present Perfect I have eaten. 過去完成,對現在有影響
Past Perfect I had eaten before he arrived. 表達過去的先後順序(兩個過去動作)
🔑 最重要的區別
不要只看形式 (verb),要看含義 (meaning)!
• Simple = 完成/習慣
• Continuous = 進行/未完成
• Perfect = 對現在有影響 / 過去或未來的先後順序

互動檢測

10 題概念題 - 檢測你是否真正理解每個時態的含義

Q1 (Present Simple): He gets up at 7 o'clock every day.
Q2 (Present Continuous): She is doing her homework now.
Q3 (Past Simple): I saw a good film last night.
Q4 (Past Continuous): I was watching TV when the phone rang.
Q5 (Present Perfect): "She has lived here for 5 years." What does this sentence mean?
Q6 (Past Perfect): When I arrived, the train had already left.
Q7 (Future Simple): There are no buses. I'll take a taxi.
Q8 (Tense Comparison): Which sentence emphasizes the result of a past action on NOW? （哪一句強調過去動作對「現在」的影響？）
Q9 (Perfect Tense Comparison): Which sentence means I am STILL living in Japan NOW?
Q10 (Perfect Tense Comparison): What is the difference between Present Perfect and Past Perfect? （現在完成式和過去完成式之間的區別是什麼？）
